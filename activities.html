Social Activities

I took part in social activities in my spare time to put my Social Work knowledge into practice.

2013
Volunteer in Federation of Youth Groups (S.P.O.Ts)

07-08/2013

I volunteered in Wuhan Changqing Garden Teen Zone.

There were varieties of activities every day, such as doing handwork, participating fire-drill and attending activities held by Popular Science Societies.

Besides, we also did research about the children who dropped out of school because of poverty. And we also contacted and found caring people who would like to help them.

We held an activity to raise and allocate fund for these children, and welcomed them to take part in the social activities in our teen zone.

2012
Volunteer Assistant in Qinduankou Urban Neighborhood Services Office

10/2012

In Qinduankou Urban Office, I mainly dealt with the daily work and offered help for the community residents, such as disseminating medical health knowledge and organising free physical examination.

At the meantime, I also investigated the living condition of eldly people, and organised social activities for them so that they could help one another and live a happy life.

Volunteer teacher in Huangtu Primary School

07-08/2012

Have you ever been without warm clothes to protect you against the cold? Have you ever been without hot lunch every day? How did you feel? It seems that nothing makes a man more humble and unsure of himself as poverty, which wrecks personal happiness and international relationships.

I did not feel keenly about it prior to my experience of volunteer teaching in Qianxinan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture of Guizhou Province. Under harsh living conditions, soon I had a fever. However, looking into the pure and trusting eyes of those kids, I felt a great sense of responsibility and persisted in preparing lessons and teaching. At evenings, we played games with them. Though they lacked of sufficient materials to keep warm and full, and had to climb rugged mountains to arrive at school, their strong thirst for knowledge made me want to keep moving forward on social work and sociology. Driven by the belief, our group spared no effort to collect all accessible clothes and school supplies for them.

2011
Volunteer in Federation of Youth Groups (S.P.O.Ts)

07-08/2011

I worked as an assistant in Wuhan Changqing Garden Teen Zone to help children with extracurricular knowledge and social abilities.

During the volunteer, I also met a child with hyperactivity. He was shy and would not play with other children. I talked to him a lot and paid much attention to him.
At first he was afraid of talking to me. But after some time, he opened his mind. He could draw some pictures and showed them to me. I noticed that what he need was caring, and this is not the least thing we can do.

I was awarded as an Excellent Project Assistant of Wuhan City.

2010
Volunteer in fund raising for a teacher suffering from leukemia

10/2010

As a member of Wu Tianxiang Group, I volunteered to raise funds at a big square.

At first, I worried about being refused, but later warmed by the love and caring hearts from the donators. I recalibrated my perspectives on the meaning of life and social work, as well as great responsibility of being a social worker to make a difference.

Besides, we also helped people build up knowledge about leukemia. Leukemia could be defeated, if we never lose hope.
